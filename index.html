A pepperoni pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 ball of pizza dough.
  • 120ml of tomato passata
  • 80g mozzarella, diced.
  • 10 slices of pepperoni.
  • 6 slices of chorizo.
  • A pinch of dry chilli flakes.
  • Flour for dusting.
  • Semolina for dusting.
  • A dash of olive oil for brushing

How to make pepperoni pizza

  1. Stretch pizza dough
  2. Add pizza dough to lightly oiled pizza stone.
  3. Add pizza sauce.
  4. Add toppings (mozzarella cheese, pepperoni slices, parmesan cheese).
  5. Bake pepperoni pizza 10-15 minutes.
  6. Top with fresh parmesan and basil.
